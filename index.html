Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score exceeds that number.

The value of each crystal is unbeknownst to you until you click on it.

Each time you begin a new game, the number values of the crystals will change.
